## PROJECT

Design a printed + PDF media kit for **Trout Tricks** — a one-person, hand-tied stillwater fly tier based in Fairmount, CO. The kit will be used for:

- Podcast pitches (fly fishing + outdoor lifestyle shows)
- Partner outreach (CO outdoor brands, cross-promo deals)
- Local press inquiries (CO outdoor magazines, regional papers)
- Wholesale shop walk-ins (Avidmax, local fly shops, Umpqua submissions)
- Investor / lender conversations
- General "send this when someone asks about Trout Tricks"

Target: a polished, **8-page PDF** (sized 8.5"×11" portrait so it can be printed for the wholesale walk-ins) AND a square-format excerpt (1080×1080 px) for Instagram-deck use.

## BRAND ASSETS

### Logo / Wordmark

- Primary lockup: `TROUT TRICKS` set in **Bebas Neue** all-caps, letter-spaced. The word "TRICKS" is colored red (`#C0392B`). The wordmark sits over a red underline rule.
- Inverted use: white text on dark, red rule unchanged.
- Logo files (PNG with transparency) are in the `images/` directory of the repo — exact filenames TBD, designer should request them.

### Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Background | Off-black | `#0F0F11` | Primary background everywhere — page bg, hero panels |
| Accent (primary) | Trout Tricks Red | `#C0392B` | Brand red — wordmark, primary CTAs, rules, accents |
| Accent (secondary) | Brand Gold | `#D4A017` | Highlight color — counter pills, scarcity callouts, accent rules |
| Body text | White | `#F0F0F0` | Body copy on dark |
| Muted text | Gray | `#888` | Secondary text, captions, meta |
| Border / divider | Dark gray | `#2A2A2A` | Card borders, dividers |
| Card bg (slight contrast) | Near-black | `#181818` | Cards on the dark background |

**Treatment notes:** designer should default to **dark background** with light text. The brand reads outdoor / serious / craft — NOT shiny / consumer / catalog. No gradients. No drop shadows. Use the gold sparingly — it's a scarcity / premium signal, not a body color.

### Typography

| Role | Family | Weights | Style |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display / headlines | **Bebas Neue** | Regular | All-caps, letter-spaced 0.05–0.12em |
| Subheads / UI / buttons | **Barlow Condensed** | 600, 700 | Often all-caps, letter-spaced 0.08–0.14em |
| Body copy | **Barlow** | 300, 400, 600 | Mixed case, normal letter-spacing |

All three are free Google Fonts. No serif anywhere.

### Photography Style

- **Subject:** trout (rainbow, brown, cutbow — held over water or in net), fly patterns on the vise, the tying bench, Colorado lakes, the angler at the water's edge.
- **Tone:** earned, gritty, real. Not stock. Not staged. Cloudy days, golden hour, indicator drift moments, weed-line water clarity.
- **Avoid:** stock photography of "white guy holding fish in a flannel," over-saturated trophy shots, drone shots of Aspen/Vail (we fish the working stillwaters, not the resort streams).
- **Fish gallery:** there are ~106 trout photos at `/fish.html` on the site if the designer wants source material.

## VOICE & TONE

**The rule:** real angler talking to real anglers. First-person. Conversational. No corporate slop.

**Banned phrases:**
- "Premium quality"
- "Industry-leading"
- "Experience the difference"
- "Hand-crafted with passion"
- "Premium fishing experience"
- "Guide-tested" *(as a lead value prop — fine as a tactical detail)*
- "Two decades of experience" or any specific tenure claim about Thomas
- Anything that sounds like a Bass Pro Shops product blurb

**Banned word swaps:**
- "Shop" → "tier" / "patterns" / "tying bench"
- "Store" → same
- "Products" → "patterns" (for flies) / "designs" (for stickers)
- "Workshop" → "tying bench"
- "Customer" → "angler" or "member"

**Ethical framing:**
- Spawn TIMING yes (when, water temps, conditions) — spawn LOCATIONS no (no GPS pin-drops, no hot-spot leaking, no naming redds).
- The Drop is a closed forum where members talk locations among themselves; public marketing copy does NOT promise location intel.

**Sample on-brand sentences:**
> "Real fish. Real water. Real patterns."
>
> "Hand-tied in Colorado. Built for anglers who actually land them."
>
> "Generic YouTube tells you how to tie a chironomid in 8 minutes. It doesn't tell you what's hitting at 17 feet on the south side of Antero this Tuesday because the water hit 52°F on Sunday."
>
> "Tied on 2x heavy wire — for the day a 24-inch cutbow takes you to the cleaner."

## NOTES FOR THE DESIGNER

- The brand is built around dark backgrounds. Don't fight that.
- Photography > illustration. Always.
- The brand red (`#C0392B`) is for accents, not for filling large surfaces.
- Don't use the gold for body text — it's a scarcity / premium-tier signal only.
- The Wordmark uses Bebas Neue with red on "TRICKS" — don't recolor it.
- Don't add an em-dash–heavy "tagline garnish" subtitle on the cover. The cover should breathe.
- When in doubt — quieter, darker, less. The audience doesn't need to be sold to; they need to feel like they found something real.
