## Posting cadence

Spread these across 10–14 days. Don't post everything in 48 hours.

- Day 1: POST 1 (carousel — builds trust first)
- Day 3–4: STORY 1 (mastermind explainer — drives DM replies)
- Day 6: POST 3 (Reel — broader reach)
- Day 8: STORY 2 (scarcity countdown — convert hesitators)
- Day 11: POST 2 (founding-10 scarcity image — final push)

Best post times for CO outdoor audience: **6–8 PM MT weekdays**, or **7–9 AM Sat/Sun**.

Hashtag set is in POST 1 caption — reuse on POST 2 and the Reel. Don't reuse on Stories (Stories don't index hashtags the same way).
