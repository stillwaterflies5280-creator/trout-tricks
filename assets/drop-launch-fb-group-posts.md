## Posting notes

- **Time of post:** 6–8 PM MT weekdays for highest CO angler engagement. Avoid Friday/Saturday.
- **Image:** if you can, attach a fish photo from your camera roll — recent CO/WY catch. Adds proof without selling.
- **Replies:** if someone asks a real question in comments, answer it in the thread. Don't pivot to DM-pushing. Group admins notice that pattern.
- **Group rules:** double-check each group's promotional rules before posting. The 20K group especially may require admin approval — DM the admins first if so.
- **Spacing:** post these out over 5–7 days, not all in one day. Algorithm hates synchronized cross-posting + members in multiple groups will see the staggered angles instead of duplicate posts.
