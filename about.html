ABOUT THOMAS

TroutTricks was built from an obsession with figuring out stillwater trout when conditions aren't easy — and most days, they aren't. I've spent countless hours at Colorado fisheries, dialing in what actually works from the bank and beyond. My approach is rooted in efficiency and consistency: fishing indicators with precision, understanding depth better than guesswork, and knowing when to slow things down versus when to trigger a reaction.

I'm especially passionate about chironomids and balanced presentations — techniques that consistently produce quality fish, including trophy-class trout over 20+ inches.

What you'll find here isn't theory — it's what I trust when I'm trying to crack tough bites, adjust to changing conditions, and stay on fish when others struggle. From carefully tied stillwater flies to thoughtfully selected gear, everything on TroutTricks is built around real experience and a relentless focus on results.

Whether you're just getting into stillwater fishing or looking to refine your edge, this is about helping you fish smarter, dial in your confidence, and ultimately catch better fish.
