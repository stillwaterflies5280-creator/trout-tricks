Fish It On — Chironomid Waters

These patterns earn fish on Colorado stillwaters where chironomids dominate the menu — Antero Reservoir, Spinney Mountain, 11 Mile Reservoir, and Delaney Buttes — plus North Park (Lake John), Steamboat-area waters, and Wyoming stillwater rotations.
