Why These Patterns Work

Chironomids — midges in their pupal and adult stage — are the dominant year-round food source for stillwater trout in Colorado. On Antero, Spinney, 11 Mile, and beyond, the angler who matches profile, color, and depth wins. Every fly here is dialed for that game: tungsten beads to sink fast, 2x heavy wire that won't straighten on a trophy fish, and color combinations proven on the South Park reservoirs.
