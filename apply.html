Questions

FAQ

What is The Drop exactly?
The Drop is a private mastermind community for serious Colorado stillwater fly anglers. You get weekly insider lake reports, member-only fly patterns, monthly live Q&A calls with a working guide, a private crew sharing real-time intel, and a member discount on Trout Tricks gear. It's the room and the playbook that cut years off your learning curve.
Who is this for?
Intermediate-to-advanced anglers who already fish Colorado stillwaters a few times a month, run an indicator or balanced setup, and want a real edge. If you're serious about getting better — and willing to share intel as well as take it — you'll fit right in.
How much does it cost?
We share membership details on the call. We screen for crew fit first — the room only works if everyone in it is serious — so the 15-minute call comes before any numbers.
What's the time commitment?
As much or as little as you want. The live Q&A is monthly. Reports and the community are there whenever you need them — most members check in a few times a week, especially before a trip. Everything live is recorded, so you never miss out.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no exit fees, cancel anytime. We'd rather have a crew that wants to be here than lock anyone in.
What if I'm a beginner?
The Drop assumes you've got the basics down — casting, rigging an indicator, reading water. If you're brand new, start with our free content and a guided trip first, then come back when you're fishing stillwater regularly. We'll tell you straight on the call if it's not the right time yet.
Are you based in Colorado?
Yes — Trout Tricks is based in Fairmount, CO, and the intel is built around the Colorado stillwater rotation: Antero, Spinney, Eleven Mile, Williams Fork, Lathrop, Delaney, Lake John, and more. The technique deep-dives travel anywhere; the lake reports are pure Colorado.
